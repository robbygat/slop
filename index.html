#play-together

prompt a game. play it together.

every slop game ships with netcode. ask Studio for "co-op" or "4-player" and it wires the multiplayer harness automatically. host a room, share a link — and live remixes sync to the whole lobby, mid-match.

1

One-prompt multiplayer

say "co-op" or "4-player" in a prompt and slop adds the netcore harness for you — no networking code, ever.

2

Drop-in rooms

one click gives you a room code and a shareable link. anyone who opens it joins the lobby instantly, peer-to-peer.

3

Live remix in the lobby

#remix-engine

Take any game. Make it weirder.

every single game on slop.game is remixable — launch games, community games, your games. the remix dock sits beside the game (never covering it), reads the real source, and patches the running game live. type it or say it out loud. in multiplayer, the host's mods hit everyone's screen.
